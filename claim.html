Claim your Pioneer
The Raccoons have taken over and are building their very own city in the metaverse. Surrounded by ocean and founded by King Lufus, the city now has 10,000 unique Raccoon residents. King Lufus provides well for his followers and in return they help to make the city prosperous by earning and spending Lufe coin tokens. The King is very proud of his followers and hopes to continue to welcome new residents in the future.
